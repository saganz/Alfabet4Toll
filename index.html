BETTER DECISIONS

Whilst planning your transformation is important, changes will continue to happen. Making the right decisions during your transformation journey is paramount. Portfolio management and improved data accuracy will give Toll a better understanding of the impact of change.

OPEX REDUCTION

New technology initiatives and the need to work closer with external partners will make Toll vulnerable to cyber security threats. While at the same time Toll must adhere to new regulations and reduce the effort to comply. If all interdependencies are understood the risk of business disruption is reduced
